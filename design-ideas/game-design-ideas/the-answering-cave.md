# The Answering Cave

## Overview
A procedurally generated cave exploration with intention set at the beginning of each journey. 
At the start of each game, the player enters an *intention or question* into a prompt. The game is randomized once the intention is applied.

Each round consisists of a number of "rooms" and a "goal" assigned at the beginning of the session.
Each room contains a puzzle. Puzzles are never failable. The choices the player makes narrate the Answer or Guidance they will recieve.

Puzzles may include:
- selecting something that seems most true/aligned
- choosing what to remove from a collection of symbols
- creating a color palette
- finding a solution through a maze with multiple solutions
- answering a multi-choice question

At the end, once all rooms have been completed, you will meet the Answering Pond and based on your game, an Answer to your intention or question will be given,
