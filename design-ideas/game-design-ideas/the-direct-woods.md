# The Direct Woods

Helps you by being way too direct.

if you enter:

“How do I find clarity?”

The cave might interpret it as:

“You want control”

or “You’re avoiding something”

or “You already know but won’t choose”

And now every room reflects that version of you, not the one you consciously asked from.

Now the interaction changes completely

You’re no longer:

solving

expressing preference

gently being mirrored

You’re:

arguing with the cave’s interpretation of you

Rooms become friction, not reflection

A room gives you only options that feel off

A path keeps looping in a way that feels unfair

An object behaves “wrong” no matter what you do

And your only power is:

insist

reject

adapt

surrender
