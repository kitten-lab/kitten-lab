Heyo, kits. 
#### Projects in this lab may appear unrelated, but I assure you, they are not.

The Kitten Lab is a place to consider cognition: Why do we think? What does it mean to know? Why do we forget and remember? And moreover, why does meaning emerge, and from where? Kitten Lab is a place of documentation and prototype exploration where each project circles the same fundamental wondering: Who are we, and why.

Projects include similar aspects across forms:

- Narrative systems
- Emergent cognition
- Player/World interactions
- Symbolic mechanics
- AI-assisted storytelling
- Amnesia and states-of-forgetting

Many projects actively converge towards the idea of persistent narrative works and procedural and AI-assisted world generation, with a focus on emergent worlds that expand as the player engages them.
