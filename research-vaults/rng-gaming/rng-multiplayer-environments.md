Betsoft Gaming produced lateral Max Quest product, featuring:

- None slot-reel RNG environment (battlefield with enemies)
- Retooling of base concept bet = spin into bet = bullet fired
- Group social play (up to 6 players vs 1 battlefield)
- Identity selection (character choice, albeit limited at launch)
- World immersion/cross over (the same heroes in different locations)

But the core issues of RNG remained:

- RNG and RTP is static

Therefore, players cannot:

- Gain improved weapons without math model inclusion
- Gain level-up perk progressions that cause mathematical impact
- Gain perceivable loss structures.

The last item in particular was limited by the perception of fiscal loss on both the players side and the operator's side, though for different reasons.

- The player perceives a sense of theft, that their bet was "stolen", should their outcome appear to lose their real money when attacked or observably dodged. 

- The operator would perceive lost potential income were the player to become unable to bet for a range of time.
